# Fitness-Facilities-Management
This analysis is intended to dive deep into Gaps & Opportunities in facilities many of us would have stepped in at least once - the Gym/Pool. Using First-hand and Secondary data collected locally, I identify areas that seek improvement and pathways to improve processes and experience for users like you and me. 


## The Case-Study is on Sport Singapore - ActiveSG Faciltiies 
Who are they? They are a statutory board of the Ministry of Culture, Community and Youth, founded in 1973
How do they serve the community? Through multi-levelled experiences, providing facilities and collaboration for all in the world of Sports.
